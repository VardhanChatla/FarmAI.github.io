Our Idea & Agricultural Sector

Chatbots are easier to approach and are availabe on service 24*7. They can perform most of the tasks that a farmer/crop care executive can, hence such service can be scaled easily. Weather alerts and forecasting along with news and updates can be reached at a higher speed than traditional sources. The chatbot will provide farmers with access to relevant information and services, which will improve their productivity and profitability. The development of a farmer-based chatbot is an innovative approach to address the challenges faced by farmers.
